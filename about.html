About Ace Painting

Established in 1985, Ace Painting has been providing customers with beautiful lasting paint jobs for commercial, industrial and residential properties.

Ace Painting is a fully licensed in accordance with CSLB (Contractors State License Board) regulations, bonded and insured full-service contractor. Our California General Contractor License Number is 693381.

We are also certified by the EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) on lead safety for renovation, repair and painting (RRP Rule). The rule addresses activities that disturb lead-based paint in target housing and child-occupied facilities.
